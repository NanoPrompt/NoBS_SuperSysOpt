NoBS_SuperSysOpt_Toolkit
-------------------
Version: 1.0
Author: Prashant&Smita (Frisco TX)

Usage:
- Run system_boost.cmd monthly or after major installs
- Use privacy_harden.ps1 after Windows updates
- Run startup_audit.bat to identify slow boot contributors
- Use driver_check.vbs quarterly to verify driver health

Logs:
All logs are saved in the /logs folder for audit and rollback purposes.
